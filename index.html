Why Krishnalaya?

Just like Lord Krishna, Kishnalaya dorm is a spiritual, punctual, determined, disciplined, transformative, well-coordinated and a very fun dormatory. Everyone here is provided with one or the other opportunities to take up. We have weekly fun dorm- activities, making us more confident to participate in other events. Our dorm is known for it's unity and the way we are harmonious and peaceful and we also write the most amount of apology letters in the WHOLE SCHOOL.This dorm motivates the students to perform and participate in many events. It is made by us, the students and our mentors, Siva sir, Padmavati ma'am and Rini ma'am. This dorm is made by our emotions and our ambitions. Even if we leave the school, no one can ever forget this very dorm..



Meet our RHP and RHMs

1. Major Siva Shanmugam Sir-

 He is the main pillar of this dorm. Without him the dorm feels different. He is the major of the NCC (National Cadet Corps.) platform in CIRS, The HOD (Head Of Department) of Sports and the one and The RHP of KRISHNALAYA. He is the one who solves all the problems, making the dorm quiet and peaceful for all of us. He is always funny at the right times, if you do anything wrong well then... apology letters it is. He makes sure everyone is performing well and gives them opportunities to train them for the hard and harsh future. He is the one behind all our achivements and programs happening in our dorm and our school.

2. Padmavati Ma'am & Rini Ma'am-

Padmavati ma'am and Rini ma'am are the RHM of Krishnalaya Downstairs and Upstairs respectively. They make sure everyone arranges all their cupboards and side lockers at all times. They mentain cleanliness and tidiness in the dorm. They tirelessly work throughout the day starting by waking us up till sending us to bed and locking the doors. They keep us safe and make us feel comfortable at all times. They help us when we need help and explain us where and when we are going wrong. They make sure we eat, sleep and stay properly while sacrificing their time and sleep ALL FOR US. To them we are very thankful.
